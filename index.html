Frequently Asked Questions

Is this an employee position?

No. This is a 100% remote, independent contractor (1099) opportunity. This structure provides you with maximum flexibility and autonomy. You are responsible for your own taxes and expenses, but you also have full control over your work and schedule.

How are my referrals and commissions tracked?

We provide you with access to a shared CRM portal. You will have a real-time, transparent dashboard to track the status of every lead you submit, from initial contact to closed deal and commission payout. We believe in 100% transparency.

Do I need to pay for any tools or software?

No. We provide the essential technology stack, including CRM access and communication channels, at no cost to you. You are responsible for your own personal business tools, such as your computer and phone.

What kind of training or support is provided?

We provide a comprehensive, self-paced onboarding program covering Shopify's products, our commission structure, and the lead submission process. We offer optional check-ins and a supportive community, but our goal is to empower you with resources, not to manage you.
